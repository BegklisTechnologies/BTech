Our Story

Founded in 2025 by a team of AI researchers and business strategists, BTech emerged from a simple observation: businesses were drowning in AI hype but lacking practical implementation paths. While others sold expensive software solutions, we took a different approach.

We believe the best AI solution is the one that works with what you already have. Our mission is to democratize AI adoption by teaching businesses how to leverage existing tools more intelligently, not by adding complexity to their tech stack.

Today, we've helped over 500 Professionals and companies worldwide achieve remarkable efficiency gains, with an average productivity increase of 340% in automated processes.
