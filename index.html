Our Story

Founded in 2025 by a team of AI researchers and software engineers, BTech emerged from a simple observation: businesses were drowning in AI hype but lacking practical implementation paths. While others sold expensive software solutions, we took a different approach.

We believe in building custom solutions that work seamlessly with your existing infrastructure. Our mission is to democratize AI and modern technology adoption by creating tailored solutions that actually solve real business problems.

Today, we've helped over 500 professionals and 22+ companies worldwide achieve remarkable efficiency gains, with an average productivity increase of 340% in automated processes.
